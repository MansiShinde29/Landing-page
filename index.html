VARIETIES

Alphonso

The Alphonso mango, also called the hapus mango, is a named mango cultivar that originated in India.

Dasheri

Dasheri mangoes are known for their sweet and fragrant pulp, which is peach-coloured and has no fibre. The skin is green to yellow-green, semi-thick, smooth.

Chaunsa

Chausa mango are one of the most popular variety of mango found in India, Commonly available in greenish-yellow color with unique taste and richness.

Totapuri

The Totapuri is named so as it resembles a parrot. Cultivated in Bengaluru, Totapuri mangoes are also known as Sandersha or Ginimoothi maavina kayi.

Langra

One of the juiciest and pulpiest varieties of mangoes, the Langra comes from the holy city of Varanasi.

Gir Kesar

The 'Gir Kesar' mango, also called Kesar, is a mango cultivar grown in the foothills of Girnar in Gujarat, western India. The mango is known for its bright orange colored pulp and was given the geographical indication status in 2011.
